About Amelia Harris Solicitors

Established with a commitment to excellence, Amelia Harris Solicitors is a premier Adelaide law firm specialising in professional litigation. Our practice is built on a foundation of integrity, rigorous legal analysis, and unwavering dedication to our clients.

Located in the prestigious Grenfell Centre at 25 Grenfell Street, Adelaide, we are strategically positioned to serve clients throughout South Australia. We understand that navigating legal disputes can be complex and challenging, which is why we offer clear, practical, and strategic advice tailored to your unique situation.

Principal Solicitor - Amelia Harris

Our firm is led by Principal Solicitor Amelia Harris, who brings extensive experience and a distinguished track record in high-stakes litigation. Her deep understanding of South Australian law, combined with a proactive and client-focused approach, ensures that Amelia Harris Solicitors consistently delivers exceptional legal solutions. Amelia is passionate about advocating for her clients and achieving justice through meticulous preparation and persuasive argumentation.
